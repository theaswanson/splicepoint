Splicepoint

Fan Site

Splicepoint was a YouTube channel known for their popular "Nerf Assassination" videos. Click here for the original playlist.

Unfortunately, the videos have since been made private. Thankfully, most of them have been recovered through the Wayback Machine and re-uploads.

To the original creators of Splicepoint: thank you for creating memorable videos from our childhood, and I hope you consider making your YouTube videos public again :)
